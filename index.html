How to play: Select either a 4×4, 5×5, or 6×6 table size. You have to bring the table to look like a checkerboard (ie. each circle has only opposite-coloured neighbours) by swiping the columns and/or rows of the table. To go back to the main menu, just swipe left in the lower portion of the screen while in-game.
